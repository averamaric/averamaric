Process Capability

Cp & Cpk Analysis

Potential Capability Cp
6.25

Cp = (USL − LSL) / (6 × σ) = 0.6 / 0.096 = 6.25

Process spread (6σ = 0.096 mm) is only 16% of the tolerance band.

Actual Capability Cpk
2.15

Cpk = min[(USL − x̄)/(3σ), (x̄ − LSL)/(3σ)] = 2.15

💡 Key insight: Cpk = 2.15 exceeds automotive standard (Cpk ≥ 1.33). Excellent control.

Calculation Chain

Step by Step

01 Annual target 50,000 phones / year (Phones-R-Us contract)
02 Working days 252 days (holidays, weekends & planned maintenance excluded)
03 Daily demand 100,000 ÷ 252 = 397 units / day
04 Available time 1 shift × 24 h = 86,400 seconds / day
05 Takt time 86,400 ÷ 397 = 218 seconds ✓
