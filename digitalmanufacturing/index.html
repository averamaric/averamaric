Background

About us

DigiCase Solutions is a specialized contract manufacturer (Tier 1 supplier) operating within Industry 4.0. Our core competency is additive manufacturing (FDM) with end-to-end digital thread integration.

The
Challenge

How to guarantee 50,000 casings behave identically to the 'master part'?

The
Risk

Geometric variability across suppliers causes assembly line jams, failures.

Our
Commitment

Statistical interchangeability and CP Lab station success without selective assembly.
