ABOUT
ME

Hi! I'm Analucia, and my passion is building the bridge between the digital and physical worlds of industry.

It all started in a childhood notebook, filled with sketches of flying robots and magnets—blueprints that later guided me to a degree in Industrial Electronics Engineering and a thrilling role as an IoT Project Manager. There, I fell in love with the puzzle of Industry 4.0: taking age-old factories and weaving them into a connected, intelligent future.

But the true revelation came when I peered into the logic of a PLC. Seeing how lines of code orchestrate entire industrial processes was a defining moment. That's when I knew I needed to get closer to the machine's core intelligence.

That's why I'm now immersed in my Master's in Robotics and Automation, deepening my hands-on skills in PLC programming, search algorithms, and industrial IoT architecture. For me, this is the ultimate toolbox to design the adaptive, efficient, and truly intelligent factories of the future.

What excites me most? Being at the crossroads where Europe's rich industrial past meets its automated future. I see a living lab—from manual processes to semi and fully automated lines—that offers a unique, holistic understanding of how to build, improve, and innovate. I want to learn from it all and contribute to that evolution.
